Lease Details

The Lease Details screen contains information about the Lease right for a property. It can be accessed from the Rights and Restrictions tab on the Property Details screen by selecting a Lease right (current, historic or pending) and clicking the toolbar button.



To create, change or terminate a lease you must first lodge an application with the appropriate service (e.g. Grant for New Lease, Transfer Lease, Termination of a Lease, etc.).
Details that can be captured for a lease include

  • General – General details of the lease including the lease number, lease term and ground rent details. SOLA provides support to calculate ground rent for new leases, but does not currently contain a ground rent payments history.
  • Lessee – The person, persons or organization that are registered as the lessee(s) for the property. Note that one of the lesseeâ€™s must be flagged as the Lessee Account Holder (using the Lessee account holder party role). This is used by the finance system to determine which lessee to send invoices to.
  • Special Conditions – All leases issued by LAA are subject statutory conditions. Where additional conditions have been established, these are recorded on the Special Conditions section.
  • Registration – The Date of Registration and the Registration Number and registration payment details such as Registration Fee, Stamp Duty and/or Transfer Duty.
