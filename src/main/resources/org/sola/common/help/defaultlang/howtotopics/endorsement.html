Endorsement

Endorsement is a transfer of the lease to new lessee(s) due to succession or inheritance. The process for the Endorsement service is similar to that for Transfer Lease, with an additional step to print the Endorsement of Succession letter after you have recorded the new lessee details and removed the original lessee details. Refer to the steps described in the Transfer Lease section for guidance.
