Register Notarial Bond

The LAA is responsible for registering bonds held over moveable property such as cars and furniture. The Register Notarial Bond service can be used to perform this registration.

The process for registering a Notarial Bond is very similar to that for recording a Power of Attorney.

Refer to the steps described in the Register Power of Attorney section for guidance.

Note that the document type to create on the application is Bond.
