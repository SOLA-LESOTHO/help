Register, Vary and Cancel Sublease Mortgage

Only properties with subleases can have sublease mortgages registered on them. The process for registering, varying and cancelling a Sublease Mortgage right is similar to that for registering, varying and cancelling a Mortgage right.

Refer to the steps described in the Register, Vary and Cancel Mortgage second for guidance.
