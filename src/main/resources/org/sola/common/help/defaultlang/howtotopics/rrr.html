Rights, Restrictions and Responsibilities (RRR)

RRR are the rights, restrictions and responsibilities that can be registered on a property. The primary right is used to indicate the main estate type for the property. In Lesotho, the primary right is Lease. The services that can be used to create, change or cancel each type of RRR are also indicated in the table.


Type Description Create Change Cancel
Lease

PRIMARY RIGHT
Indicates the property is subject to a lease
  • Grant of New Lease
  • Registration On Lease
  • Change of Lessee Name(s)
  • Endorsement
  • Renewal of Lease
  • Transfer Lease
  • Vary Lease
  • Registration On Lease
  • Surrender of Lease
  • Termination of Lease
  • Registration on Lease
Mortgage Indicates the property is subject to a mortgage.
  • Register Mortgage
  • Cession of Mortgage
  • Cancel Mortgage
Servitude Indicates the property is subject to or benefits from an easement.
  • Register Servitude
  • Vary Servitude
  • Cancel Servitude
Sublease Indicates the property is subject to a sublease.
  • New Sublease
  • Registration on Lease
  • Vary Sublease
  • Registration on Lease
  • Cancel Sublease
  • Registration on Lease
Sublease Mortgage Indicates the sublease is subject to a mortgage.
  • Register Mortgage (where property has a sublease)
  • Registration on Lease
  • Cession of Mortgage
  • Cancel Mortgage
