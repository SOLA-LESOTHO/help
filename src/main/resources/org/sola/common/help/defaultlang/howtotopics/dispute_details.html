Disputes and Court Cases Details

Disputes between lessees and court cases where the LAA is a party can be recorded on the Disputes and Court Cases screen. This screen can be accessed by clicking Disputes & Court Cases toolbar icon.



The Disputes and Court Processes screen can capture the plot that is the subject of the dispute, summary details about the dispute including the parties involved, any documents supporting the dispute as well as a history of comments relating to the dispute.
Once the dispute or legal challenge is resolved, the dispute record can be closed using the Complete Dispute button.

Dispute Search

To locate details for an existing dispute or court process, the Dispute Search form can be used. This is accessible by clicking the Search button on the Dispute and Court Cases form.
